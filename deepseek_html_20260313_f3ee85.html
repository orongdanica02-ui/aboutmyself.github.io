my story, briefly

I grew up between the mountains and the coast — that probably explains why i need both creative focus and wide open spaces. After studying communication design and computer science, i realised my happy place is where logic meets empathy: designing experiences that actually help people.

For the past few years i’ve worked as a product designer & front-end tinkerer. I love the moment when a tricky problem becomes a simple, elegant solution. Outside work, I’m probably reading sci‑fi, learning to play the ukulele (badly), or planning a road trip with my dog.

Right now I’m diving deeper into interactive storytelling and inclusive design. I believe the web can be both beautiful and kind — and i’m here for it.

— alex
